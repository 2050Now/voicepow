📱 Background recording with Apple Shortcuts
1Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone
2Tap + → New Shortcut → Add Action
3Search "Record Audio" → select it → set "When run" to Immediately
4Add action "Save File" → save to iCloud Drive / Voice Memos folder
5Tap the shortcut name → Add to Home Screen
6Now: tap shortcut → records in background with mic indicator → come back here and upload the file
The orange mic icon in your status bar confirms recording is active. Come back to VoiceNote and tap "Upload audio file" when done.
